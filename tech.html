ABOUT APP

Be incharge of your poultry at all times

Why fowlX?

fowlX is a farming digital application which is created basically for the agricultural sector of Uganda. It is aimed at optimizing farming processes with a number of smart features that are available on computers. This application is meant aid poultry farmers to meet the continuously growing demand of chicken in Uganda.

Main Importance

It keeps track of:
  • Egg collection
  • Bird population
  • Vaccinations and treatments
  • Keeps track of stock levels(poultry feeds)
  • Data's concerning bird motarlity is maintained in our systems

Overview

fowlX is an easy to use with technology which enables farmers to manage every aspect of their poultry farm. It is capable of monitoring a variety of breeds ranging form broilers to layers and hybrids. Our system provides remote and real-ttime monitoring. Our technology is simple to implement and use, and features an intuitive dashboard display. The application is designed to record all transactions and activities involved in a poultry farm as well as the solution integrates daily reports, sales, purchases.
It also includes flock performance of the birds, feed formulation depending on the breed and stage of the birds. Frequent reminders will always alert the farmer to vaccinate the birds, check on the stock of feeds when need be, monitor the sales pattern so as to maximize profits.
When chicks are included into the system, it automatically updates the system and keeps track of their health in terms of feeding and vaccination.

Major Benefits of FowlX application

The farmers will always be up to date about their stock levels because they will always be updated by the app and this will reduce unnecessary purchase of supplies and late purchase of supplies.
The farmers will have an advanced and efficient record keeping system and this will lead to an improvement in decision making as well as tax evaluation and monitoring of progress on the farm.
The farmers will have a clear overview of their sales such that the farmer knows what to do if sales increase in order to maintain them and to revamp them when they drop.
The farmers will always be notified about the regular vaccination schedules, deworming and check-up of the birds.
This regularly updated data input in the system will enable the farmer make better decisions concerning the farm
